About me

My name is Victor Sarmiento. I am a Software Engineer based out of Las Vegas, Nevada. After spending some time at University, I was hired on to be a Software Engieer Contractor at Silversheet Medical Credentialing. It was my first and only job being recognized as a Software Engineer. I've got to say that the work has been very helpful in making connections to different concepts I didn't have a full grasp on. I'm looking forward to finishing this journey and having an even stronger foundation for web development by the end of this. After finishing the program, I hope to migrate to Washington or Texas to be a software engineer and make an impact at my future employer.
